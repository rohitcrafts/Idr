About IDR

Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is dedicated to preparing the next generation of professionals who can understand and manage digital, cyber and AI-driven risks.

IDR bridges the gap between technology innovation and risk governance by providing practical education, industry collaboration and advanced research in cybersecurity, artificial intelligence governance and digital compliance.
